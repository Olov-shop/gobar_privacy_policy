Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2025

App Name: GoBar
Developer: IkpyDev

Overview

GoBar is a mobile application that allows users to find and book nearby barbers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the GoBar app.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and optional profile photo when registering.
  • Location Data: Used to find nearby barbers and improve location-based services (with your permission).
  • Booking History: We store your appointment history for your convenience.
  • App Usage Info: Data such as features used, ratings given, and errors encountered to improve the app.

How We Use the Information

  • To display available barbers near you.
  • To manage your bookings and send appointment details to barbers.
  • To track and show your order history.
  • To display barber details including availability and working hours.
  • To improve and maintain app performance and service quality.

Barber Information

Users can view detailed barber information such as:

  • Barber name and salon details (if any)
  • Location and directions on map
  • Working hours (opening/closing times)
  • Real-time availability
  • Service list and pricing
  • User reviews and ratings

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Location: To suggest barbers nearby.
  • Internet access: For real-time updates and communication with servers.
  • Notifications: To send reminders and booking status updates.

Data Security

We take user privacy seriously and store all data securely. We do not share your personal information with third parties unless legally required or with your consent.
